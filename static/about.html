LITTLE WONDER

This project began as a personal way of processing David Bowie's passing, and bsking in his songs, but it turned into an experiment that a few others liked exploring too.

Above all, it is an examination of persona. David Bowie continually changed, gifting us many new characters and incredible songs, during a beloved career.

The objective of the app is to see if there is a correlation between his written lyrics and his visual personas.

We take the lyrics from albums that correspond to each persona/period and run personality insights on them. Clicking on one of the drawings of Bowie on the main view opens a page that links like Twitter for that persona.

The 'tweets' contain sankey diagrams that break down the big five personality traits and a little bar chart that shows the combined trait measurements.

Displaying this as a Twitter page emphasizes the theme of persona. This app is also trying to honor the personas in a fun way, through mining the data within them, it is trying share an understanding of the personas, and songs over time.

From the main view you can also find a link to a page which shows the big five traits over time, and per persona.

A few things stood out to me in this data. For instance, in 1975 - the thin white duke period - Bowie himself suggested this was his darkest most unlike able persona - you can see the openness trait dips significantly.

Curiously for the same period this was when his lyrics were most agreeable.

His lyrics were not agreeable across the personas at all - Bowie really challenged authority - maybe why he appealed so much. There are also controls to toggle between the contributing sub-traits.

This view shows how we can track personality over time.

Finally there is a view to find your inner Bowie. Reading your tweets and mapping to the closest markers of one of the personas. We show this comparison in overlapping polar charts. We use a range of visualization to examine the personalities from different angles.

This site was hand crafted with css/html - no jquery, no bootstrap ... Just plain css flex layouts ...

The main Bowie images were all drawn in illustrator based on the same 'nude' Bowie model

Used Pantone colors of spring 2016 as a palette

Wanted a consistent, colorful, fun but respectful way to navigate his personalities over time.
